Kristin Joelle Nugent

Kristin grew up in the US and has been involved in teaching in some form since she was about 13 when she began teaching swimming lessons over summer breaks. She has spent 16 of her 25 years teaching in international schools in Europe, South America and Asia. Kristin is certified to teach PK-8 and has taught most grades and subjects from 1-8, but loves middle school most of all. She is currently teaching 6th grade math, science and ethics, but taught grades 7 and 8 math for three years in her current school as well. She has many years of experience in humanities and has also loved teaching science in middle school. Additionally, she has nearly a decade of upper ES experience. She coaches soccer and swimming now, but has also coached field hockey for years as well as basketball occasionally. At home she reads as much as she can, to herself as well as to Lucas!
