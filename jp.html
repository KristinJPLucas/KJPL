Juan Pablo Vergara Lara

Juan Pablo was born in Chile, but spent 13 years of his childhood in Zaire, Africa where he attended a Belgian international school. Though he began his career in engineering, he caught the teaching bug first as a TA in college, and later teaching GRE preparation courses. Juan Pablo has degrees in education and engineering and holds secondary math certification. He currently teaches all levels of HS math and a TOK class, and wrote the HS curriculum at our current school utilizing a standards based approach to grading and reporting. He has extensive experience in all levels of IB. He, too, coaches soccer as well as volleyball and basketball. Beyond sports he has run a film-making club and helped start our MUN club. Both in and out of school he loves spending time keeping up with the latest in technology and playing soccer and other sports.
